About Market Gate


Located in the beating heart of Newton Abbot, Market Gate has it all.


Whether it's a trip down to the local, or an all-day special occasion, Market Gate has the drinks, the food, the music and the sport, meaning you can count on us for top-class entertainment. Our bar then turns into the hottest nightclub in town, with our talented resident DJs.


We're really friendly too! Market Gate has the appeal of your local boozer, but with many more strings to our bow. As well as our in-house pizzas, cocktail towers, dancefloor and the day's biggest match, we also deliver food and drink so you can bring the party home.


With everything served 7 days a week, we know how to have fun around here.

Music


Lights down. Tunes up.


Back with a bang, our club nights are a hit every weekend. Our speakers pump out tech and house, while you drink from our unbelievable promotions.


We also have live music, with special guests performing, so if you want a boogie, Market Gate can provide the inspiration for those dance moves!

Drinks


We pride ourselves on our choice of drinks.


Market Gate's finest selection of drinks include Grey Goose and Dom Perignon, a sure way to add a classy touch to your night. Yet we also have Jaegerbombs and Hooch, if you're looking to start your party.


Our bar and club serve these and a wide selection, and with our Booze Brothers, they also get delivered straight to your door.

Food


Quick snack? Or full meal?


If you're looking for a stone-fired, hand-pulled pizza with an incredible choice of toppings, Benji's is the place to be.


We also have gourmet burgers, and TWELVE options of munchie boxes, including chicken nuggets, onion rings, fries and more. We have veggie options too.


As with our drinks, we offer delivery, collection, and eat-in for all our food, so if you're looking for options to satisfy your hunger and have fun, Market Gate is the one that you want.

Sports Bar


Any pint for any occasion.


With Sky and BT Sports, and a regular showing of the big fights and anticipated matches, you can count on us to deliver highs and lows of game day.


Market Gate can relax you with a pint after a long day at work, or start the day off with a pint and see where the night takes you!
